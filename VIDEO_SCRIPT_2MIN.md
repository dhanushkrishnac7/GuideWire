# Humsafar - 2 Minute Video Script

**[0:00 - 0:10] Opening**
"Welcome to Humsafar - an AI-powered parametric insurance platform protecting India's 5 million delivery workers from income loss due to weather and traffic disruptions."

**[0:10 - 0:25] Onboarding Screen**
"The journey starts with our onboarding screen where delivery partners select their fleet - whether they work for Zomato, Swiggy, Blinkit, or other platforms. They enter their delivery ID and choose their coverage plan. The interface is designed for quick signup with no paperwork required - protection starts at just 5 rupees per day."

**[0:25 - 0:45] Home Dashboard**
"The home dashboard is the command center. At the top, workers see their protected income for the week and active coverage status. The AI Safety Triggers section shows real-time conditions - heavy rain, extreme heat, or traffic gridlock. When conditions cross thresholds, the system automatically detects them. Below, workers see their recent automatic payouts with timestamps showing instant approval. Everything updates live as conditions change."

**[0:45 - 1:05] Claims Screen**
"The claims screen demonstrates our zero-touch automation. When a disruption is detected, the parametric verification pipeline runs in milliseconds. First, environmental data is validated using a Random Forest model. Then, device GPS is authenticated using Isolation Forest to prevent fraud. Finally, the payout sequence initiates automatically. Workers see each verification step with timestamps - the entire process takes under one second. Successful claims show instant UPI transfers with the exact payout amount."

**[1:05 - 1:20] Analytics Screen**
"The analytics screen provides insights into protected earnings. Workers see their total income saved, success rate, and claims settled. The weekly resilience chart shows protected versus unprotected working hours across the week. Smart tips appear based on patterns - for example, suggesting workers enable Rain Shield during evening hours when other partners earned more."

**[1:20 - 1:35] Simulation Screen**
"For demonstration purposes, the simulation screen lets us trigger conditions manually. Adjust rainfall intensity, temperature, air quality, flood alerts, or strike status. When values cross thresholds, watch the notification flow - detecting, verifying eligibility, processing payout, and confirming payment received. The system includes GPS spoofing detection to prevent fraud."

**[1:35 - 1:50] Profile Screen**
"The profile screen shows the worker's GScore - a risk profiling metric based on work history, zone, and claim patterns. Workers see their weekly premium, policy details, and coverage status. The exclusions section clearly states what's not covered - this is income protection only, not health or accident insurance. Workers can access their policy, claims history, and analytics from here."

**[1:50 - 2:00] Closing**
"Humsafar provides instant, automatic income protection without manual claims. Payouts happen in seconds when parametric conditions are met. The system is transparent, fraud-resistant, and designed to scale across India's entire gig economy. Thank you."

---

## Key Points Covered:
- Onboarding: Fleet selection, quick signup, no paperwork
- Home: Real-time trigger monitoring, automatic payout history
- Claims: Parametric verification pipeline, instant settlements
- Analytics: Protected earnings insights, weekly resilience tracking
- Simulation: Demo environment for testing triggers and fraud detection
- Profile: GScore, premium details, policy management

## Technical Highlights Mentioned:
- Random Forest for environmental validation
- Isolation Forest for GPS fraud detection
- Sub-second verification pipeline
- Instant UPI transfers
- Zero-touch automation
- Real-time condition monitoring

**Total Duration: Exactly 2 minutes**
